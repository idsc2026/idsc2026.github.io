## 🎯 The Mission: Mathematics for Champions

This challenge is built for teams that refuse to settle for average.

Mathematics is not just theory — it is precision.  
Data is not just information — it is power.

Your mission is to transform complex biomedical data into models that are:
- Scientifically rigorous  
- Highly accurate  
- Interpretable and ethical  
- Clinically meaningful  

This is **Mathematics for Hope in Healthcare** — where excellence meets impact.

---

## 💻 The Challenge: Code That Makes a Difference

Healthcare decisions depend on identifying subtle patterns in physiological signals and medical images.

Biomedical data is noisy.  
Medical decisions are sensitive.  
Performance alone is not enough.

Your model must be:
- Robust  
- Transparent  
- Reproducible  
- Responsible  

Champions are not those who chase accuracy alone —  
but those who build solutions that can stand in the real world.

---

## 📂 Official Datasets (Choose Your Arena)

Teams must use **at least one** of the official PhysioNet datasets below.  
External labeled datasets are strictly prohibited.

### 🧠 bigP3BCI (P300-based Brain–Computer Interface)
- EEG signals and event markers  
- Objective: P300 detection, BCI decoding, cross-session generalization  

### ❤️ Brugada-HUCA (12-Lead ECG for Brugada Syndrome)
- 12-lead ECG recordings  
- Objective: Classify Brugada syndrome vs. normal controls  

### ☢️ Myocardial Perfusion SPECT
- Cardiac nuclear imaging data  
- Objective: Segmentation, preprocessing, or clinical risk modeling  

### 👁️ HYGD (Hillel Yaffe Glaucoma Dataset)
- Retinal fundus images  
- Objective: Glaucoma detection and quality-aware modeling  

---

## 📦 Final Deliverables

Every team must submit:

🛠 **Validated Pipeline**  
Strict train/validation/test separation  

🔍 **Interpretability Component**  
Feature importance, saliency maps, or model explanations  

🗣 **Clinical & Ethical Discussion**  
Limitations, fairness, deployment considerations  

📁 **Reproducible Repository**  
Fully documented preprocessing and modeling workflow
