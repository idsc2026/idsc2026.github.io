We invite students from across the globe to join the **International Data Science Challenge (IDSC) 2026** and use mathematics to bring hope to healthcare!

### Eligibility & Team Formation
* **Who can join:** Open to Undergraduate and Postgraduate students globally.
* **Team Size:** 2 to 4 members per team.
* **Team Rules:** Teams must maintain the same composition from registration through the final round. Cross-university teams are highly encouraged!

### Important Registration Dates
* **Official Launch & Registration Opens:** 25 February 2026
* **Registration Deadline:** 10 March 2026
* **Stage 1 Submission Deadline:** 25 March 2026
